Qyint Decision Making Process

Note:

Some Processes might be changed in the Future. Licensing will never change.

Question

How do Questions get introduced to the Staff Team in the first place?
If a Staff Member sends a Question to the Administrators, it cannot be declined.
If a User wants to change something, they may request a Change to the Staff Team.
Staff may decline a Request by a regular User.

Election

Who is allowed to make Decisions?
There are differend Kinds of Elections.
Administrator Elections (only Admins vote)
Staff Elections (the entire Staff Team votes)
Administrator Elections last for a Week or until all Administrators have voted.
Staff Elections last for two Weeks or until all Staff Members have voted.
Anyone may start a Poll, for example on Discord, to ask for the Users Opinions.
Staff can decide for themselves if they want to abide by the Peoples Rule or make their own Decision.

§1 - Staff Team

How is the Staff Team built? ...
Administrators may decide to promote a User to a Staff Position.
Such a Request will be voted on by the Administrators.
If 2/3 of the Administrator Team decide to promote a User to a Position, they will be promoted.
Any User may request the Demotion of a Staff Member, this will also be voted on by the Administrators with a 2/3 Majority deciding.
Such a Request may not be declined.

Administrators may decide to promote a Staff Member to an Administrator Position.
Such a Request will be voted on by the entire Staff Team.
100% of the Staff Team have to vote in favor of the Promotion for them to be admitted into the Position.

§2 - Punishments

How is a Punishment decided on? ...
Moderators and higher-positioned Staff Members decide on a Punishment.
Administrators may decide to change a Punishment, for example by revoking a Ban. (It is the punished Users responsibility to message Administrators about appeals)
If Administrators cannot decide on what Punishment is valid, it will be voted on by the entire Staff Team.
Only Administrators may delete a Users Data (as in Data stored on Qyint Servers) after voting on it with a 2/3 Majority.

§3 - Service Management

Who owns the Qyint Servers? ...
The Maintainer of a Service owns 100% of it. (Unless they, for example, share it with another Maintainer.) Usually the Administrators maintain every Qyint Service.
Services not maintained by Administrators have to be marked as such.
Note: We can never guarantee the Stability or Security of our own Services, but we try to keep good Security practices.
We will never share your Data with third parties or use it for malicious purposes.
How youre gonna know? Never. We could share the Data we collect, which is only what you share, at any Moment, but we promise not to.
Honesty is important. You have no reason to trust us.

§4 - Repository Management

How are changes decided on?
The Creator of a Repository in the Qyint-Community Github is the Owner. (Unless they, for example, transfer Ownership.)
There are two Ways to permit a Change to a Repository.
Option 1: Through the Staff Team
Just like any other Decision, a Change requested by the Staff Team will be decided on.
Note: Important Decisions (such as Security Updates) will only be decided on by the Administrators.
Finally, if the Decision passes through the Staff Team, the Maintainer of the Respository may decide to approve or deny the Change.
Option 2: Through the Maintainer
The Maintainer may decide to accept a Change.
But the Change can be rejected retrospectively by the Staff Team.
If a Maintainer decides to push malicious Updates or does not listen to the Majority Vote of the Staff Team, they may be sanctioned.
Sancions may be decided on by the Administrators.
Note: Fixing Typos does not require authorisation.
