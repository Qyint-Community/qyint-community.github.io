May 1st, May Day

On this Day in 1886, american Labor Unions called for General Strike. They wanted an eight-hour Work Day, instead of the previous twelve.
Ever since, May Day was recognized as an important Day in leftist History.
In Germany, der "Tag der Arbeit" or "Maifaiertag" was first introduced by the Weimar Government for 1919;
The Nazis later appropriated this Holiday, as they did with many leftist Symbols. But it didnt have much in common with the actual May Day.
The Soviets and the West reintroduced the actual May Day in Germany after the second World War.
