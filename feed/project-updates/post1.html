Why we use RSS Feeds

The Internet should be free. Free of charge and free as in 'Freedom'.
RSS Feeds allow you to choose what and how to consume.
RSS is completely free. Just download an RSS Reader of your Choice, add the Feeds you want and youre good to go!
The Qyint Community does not want to force our Users to use a certain Service to recieve Updates. So we have decided to host an RSS Feed.

How to use our RSS Feeds

Step 1: Download an RSS Reader
Our Recommendations: Fluent Reader (for Desktop), Feeder (for Mobile)
Both are free and open source.

Step 2: Add our Feeds
Open the Community Feed, click on the Feeds you want to add and copy-paste the Link into your Reader!
