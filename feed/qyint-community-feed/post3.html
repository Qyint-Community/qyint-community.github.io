"Memember"-Status

Being a Qyint Member allows you to participate in General Assemblies, Members will be granted their own Personal Website and potentially more.

General Assembly

The Decision Making Process was changed. Now there is a General Assembly every month.
Every Qyint Member will be able to participate in a General Assembly through Discord or Matrix.
