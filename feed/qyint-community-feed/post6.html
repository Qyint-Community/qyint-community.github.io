Greetings,

More than Half of all Qyint Users are now on Revolt and Matrix.

- Voice Chats will be deleted, as they can no longer be moderated. (use Jitsi for VC)

- We will no longer grant Roles to Discord Users.
- We will no longer add/remove/update any Features of the Discord, including Channels, Roles and Emojis.

Soon, we will also remove all Moderators and the Link to the Server;
and the entire Server in the Future.
We are also seeking to improve Quality of Life on Matrix, maybe with a Bridge from Revolt to Matrix directly.

This Decision may not be overwritten by the General Assembly, and may not be challenged democratically. It is final.
