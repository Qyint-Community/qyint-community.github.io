The Discord Server will be archived.

Greetings!

The General Assembly has unanimously decided to archive the Discord.

You will no longer be able to interact with our Community through Discord.
All Channels have been set to private and the Bridges were taken down.
Administrators will still have Access to the Channels if we need anything.

The Matrix Space has also been removed,
as it isnt used by anyone and maintaining it would be a Waste of Time.

Why?

We have approved the new Manifesto,
which outlines our Values and Goals.
Those of the Discord Administration contradict ours.
It is undemocratic and only interested in Profit,
instead of trying to provide a good Service.
The Discord Userbase is fed up with its Administration.
Most of our Users already use democratic and copyleft Alternatives,
so we dont see a Reason to keep the Discord running.
If you want to learn more, read our Manifesto or ask us!

What now?

We will keep on doing our Stuff on Revolt.
Revolt is a copyleft Alternative to Discord.
It is not interested in removing good Features for Shareholder Profit,
and even if it were, everyone can make their own Copy.
It offers absolute Freedom and Privacy.
And it even has Features that Discord doesnt (or locks behind a Paywall)
like Color Gradients, animated Emojis everywhere, KaTeX Text Formatting,
Server Discovery for everyone, Server Identities, Banners and much more.
Allthough Revolt is not Feature Complete, the Developers are working hard
on updating it - and with the new Clients, Voice Chats and E2EE coming,
It will become a perfect Alternative to Discord.

If you have any more Questions, you can always ask us!
