New Manifesto approved

Greetings!

We are proud to announce that the new Manifesto has been approved by the General Assembly.

The Manifesto is a Document outlining our Values, Goals and Rules.
Previously, we had a Website for Rules and one explaining our Democracy.
This has now changed. Not only is everything a single Document now,
The Rules and our democratic Process have also changed.

Our Manifesto revolves around three Key Pillars:
Copyleft and Freedom;
Democracy and Transparency;
Peaceful Revolution
It also contains our new Rules.
This also means all our Projects will change their License to GPLv3

Long live the Revolution!
